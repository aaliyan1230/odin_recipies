PIZZA

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta

Ingredients

  • Mushrooms
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Oil
  • Veggies

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven at 350 degree farhenheit for about 20 minutes.
  2. Make you own dough from flour, by adding appropriate amount of yeast.
  3. Apply tomato sauce on the rolled dough for pizza.
  4. Add toppings, and a layer of cheese over the pizza.
  5. Bake the Pizza in preheated oven for 20 minutes at 350 degree farhenheit.
