FRIED RICE

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Meat/Chicken
  • Veggies
  • Soysauce and oyster sauce

Steps:

  1. Chop the meat and veggies.
  2. Season the meat and prepare egg and rice.
  3. Mix the ingredientswith rice.
  4. Add Soy sauce and oyster sauce to taste.
  5. Serve with added veggieson top, and sauce to taste.
